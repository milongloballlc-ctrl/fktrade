Shipping

Last updated: July 15, 2026

FKTrade LLC ships to customers across the United States through the online marketplaces where our products are listed. Shipping timelines, carriers, and costs for a specific order are set by the marketplace and fulfillment method used for that listing. The information below reflects our general shipping standards.

1. Processing time

Orders are typically processed and handed to a carrier within 1–2 business days of purchase confirmation, excluding weekends and US federal holidays.

2. Carriers

Depending on the item, destination, and fulfillment method, orders ship via USPS, UPS, or FedEx, or through the marketplace's own logistics network where the listing is fulfilled by that marketplace.

3. Coverage area

We currently ship to all 50 US states. We do not offer international shipping at this time.

4. Delivery estimates

Standard delivery typically takes 3–7 business days after an order ships, depending on the carrier, service level, and destination. The delivery estimate shown at checkout on the marketplace is the most accurate for a given order.

5. Tracking

Tracking information is provided through the marketplace order page once a shipping label is created, and updates as the carrier scans the package in transit.

6. Shipping costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout on the marketplace based on the item, destination, and service level selected, and may be included in the item price for some listings.

7. Contact

Questions about shipping can be sent to info@fktrade.llc.
