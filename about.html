How we work

We keep our catalog intentionally focused: fewer listings, each one vetted and in demand, rather than a wide but unfiltered range.

Every product is picked because we'd be comfortable standing behind it — not because it filled a category.

Our approach

FKTrade LLC sources and sells everyday goods across home, tools, pet supplies, toys, beauty, and office products. Rather than chasing every category, we stay within a set of verticals we know well and can vet properly before adding a product to the catalog.

Shipping and support

Orders ship across the US, with free shipping once your cart reaches $49. If you have a question about an order or a product, we reply within one to two business days.
